About the School

Ahmad Grammar & Islamic School serves students in classes 1 through 5. The school focuses on foundational literacy and numeracy, basic science, social studies, and Islamic education. Our approach balances modern teaching methods with moral and religious instruction to develop well-rounded children.

Praises for Our Teachers

Our teachers are the backbone of the school — patient, dedicated, and trained to support young learners. A few highlights:

  • Patient guidance that helps every student progress at their own pace.
  • Strong focus on reading, handwriting, and fundamental math skills.
  • Integration of Islamic morals and ethics into daily lessons.
  • Regular communication with parents to track student progress.
  • Warm classroom environment that encourages curiosity and respect.

Classes Offered (1 — 5)

We currently offer the following classes and a sample of their curricular focus:

Class 1

Foundational literacy, introduction to numbers, basic Quranic recitation.

Class 2

Building reading fluency, basic mathematics, introductory Islamiat lessons.

Class 3

Expanded language skills, arithmetic, general knowledge.

Class 4

More advanced numeracy and comprehension, science basics.

Class 5

Preparation for higher primary, strengthened study habits, moral education.
